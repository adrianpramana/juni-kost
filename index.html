Boarding Houses History

Sutar & Juni Kost

Starting from working hard as a teacher, to having goals and successful business goals.

Sutar & Juni Kost is a boarding facility located in the Renon area, Denpasar Regency, Bali Province. In this case, Sutar boarding house is the first facility established by a chef and teacher named I Wayan Sutarjana and Ni Nengah Juniati in 2020. After that, from the boarding house business, the family built a new boarding house business called June Kost in 2022. Thus, the goal that the founder wants to achieve is to create boarding houses with complete and strategic facilities and of course, prioritize customer satisfaction.
